Packaging, done differently

Hot food shouldn't live in plastic.

Most delivery meals sit in single-use plastic containers — trapping heat and moisture in a way that affects both taste and health. We package every Tiffn meal in food-safe, sustainable containers built to keep food fresher, for you and the planet.
